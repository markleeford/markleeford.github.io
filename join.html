There is no typical ASJ member.

Some of us are Japanese.
Others of us have lived in Japan for decades.
And yet others are just passing through.

We are a family of doctors, business people, diplomats, professors,
students, artists, and just about anyone else that you can imagine.

What unites us—as with our founders 150 years ago—is our spirit
of exploration and natural curiosity about Asia, most especially Japan.
We are hooked on the excitment of discovery and of sharing discoveries. 

If you think ASJ might be for you, please contact us.
We will be delighted to listen to your interests and tell you more about ASJ.

ALL ABOUT ASJ

REGULAR MEMBER (¥11,000/year)

Purchase of a Regular Membership is the way for individuals to join ASJ.

Benefits include: voting rights, admission to all lectures and events (subject to seating availability), one copy of "The Transactions of the Asiatic Society of Japan", ASJ Bulletin (newsletter), access to ASJ's digital archive of the Transactions, complimentary tickets to exhibitions at museums and galleries (subject to availability).

Options:

Sustaining Member (+¥5,000 p.a.). ASJ depends on the generosity of its members for much of its funding. ASJ recognizes members who contribute ¥5,000 or more above their annual Regular Member fee as Sustaining Members in the Transactions. 

Annual Entrance Fee for Spouse of Regular Member (+¥4,000 p.a.). For an entrance fee of ¥4,000 per year, spouses of Regular Members gain admission to all lectures, events, and complimentary exhibition tickets (subject to availability). Those covered by Annual Entrance Fee for Spouse are not members and may not vote.

Life Member (¥150,000). For a one-time fee of ¥150,000, you may obtain a Regular Membership for life.

Student Rate (¥4,200 p.a.). Applicants who submit proof of registration at a university, college, or high school may purchase a Regular Membership at the student rate.  

CORPORATE MEMBER (¥100,000/year )

Purchase of a Corporate Membership is the way for companies, universities, colleges, research institutions, schools, not-for-profit organizations, and libraries to Join ASJ. In doing so, up to three representatives may be registered as Corporate Members; they will enjoy all the benefits of Regular Members. Membership in ASJ is an excellent way for your employees to develop a more nuanced understanding of Japan while enhancing relationships by means of a shared interest. 

SPECIAL WAIVER OF FEE

ASJ strives to provide universal access regardless of personal economic circumstances. Under certain conditions, a one-time fee waiver is granted to those in hardship. Please contact the ASJ Office for information.
