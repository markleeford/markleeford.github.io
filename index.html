CALL FOR PAPERS: "THE TRANSACTIONS"
Dr. Erich Berendt and Professor Kei Yamanaka, Editors
The Transactions editorial committee welcomes the submission of research papers, short papers, essays, brief reports or commentaries to the editors, and book reviews by September 1, 2019. Now in its 147th year of publication, The Transactions has contained papers from the following fields as related to Japan:

History
Biography
Sociology, Anthropology, and Ethnology
Government, Politics, and Law
Economics and Business
Natural Sciences
Geography
Mythology, Religion, and Philosophy
Language
Literature
Art and Technology

How to Submit Your Paper
All submissions should be e-mailed to the Editors (erich.berendt@asjapan.org and kei.yamanaka@asjapan.org) with the subject line “Transactions Manuscript."

Stylistic Guidelines
Please follow the guidelines, below:

• Manuscripts should be submitted in an MS Word file (12-point Times New Roman) or a text file.
• Full papers should be under 12,000 words, including an abstract (~200 words) and a list of key words or phrases.
• Short papers, essays, brief reports or commentaries to the editors, and book reviews should be under 6,000 words. An abstract and key words are not required.
• Please incude a brief biography of the author (under 100 words).
• For issues not covered in this guide, please refer to the APA style book.

Review Process
Research papers will be reviewed in two stages, first by the editorial committee, and if deemed necessary by blind peer review. Notification of acceptance will be made within four weeks of submission.
